Sharing makes VR better

ShareVR is a plugin SDK that helps capture and share awesome VR experience for everyone to enjoy. A voice-commanded 3rd-person camera integrated by our plugin places you at the center of the experience. Players can share videos of adventures instantly on social media with friends. No complicated setup. Keep the headset on and start sharing.

  • • Voice activated
  • • 3rd-person Avatar
  • • 1-minute setup
  • • No jittery videos
